Mental Health Hotlines

Crisis Hotlines:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741 (24/7 crisis support via text message)

Additional Hotlines:

  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI): 1-800-950-NAMI (1-800-950-6264)
  • Mental Health America (MHA): 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255)
  • Veterans Crisis Line: 1-800-273-8255 and press 1 (for veterans, service members, and their families)
  • Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA): 1-800-662-HELP (1-800-662-4357)
  • Depression and Bipolar Support Alliance (DBSA): 1-800-826-3632
